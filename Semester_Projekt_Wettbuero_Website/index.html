Understanding different Races

Horse racing is a thrilling sport where horses, ridden by jockeys, compete over set distances on flat tracks or over obstacles.

Dograces are competitive events where dogs, usually greyhounds, race around a track to determine the fastest runner.

A snail race is a playful competition where snails race each other over a short distance, with the slowest-moving snail reaching the finish line first.

Game Rules

Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to Race Royale Bets! To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all our users, please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our rules and guidelines. By participating in our betting platform, you agree to adhere to the following rules:



1. Account Registration

Accurate Information:
When registering for an account, you must provide accurate and truthful information. Any attempt to create multiple accounts or provide false information may result in suspension or termination of your account.


Age Requirement:
Users must be at least 18 years old to participate. Verification of age may be required.



2. Betting Guidelines

Placing Bets:
Bets can only be placed on upcoming races. Once a race has started, no further bets will be accepted.


Single Bet Per Race:
Each user is allowed to place only one bet per race. Multiple bets on the same race are not permitted.


Bet Amount:
The amount of the bet must not exceed the available balance in your account. Our system will verify your balance before accepting a bet.



3. Race Information and Betting

Race Types:
Bets can be placed on horse races, dog races, and snail races. Each race type has unique attributes and characteristics.


Race Schedule:
Races are scheduled and randomly generated by our algorithm. This ensures a fair and dynamic betting environment.


Participant Odds:
Each participant in a race has specific odds, reflecting their chances of winning. These odds are used to calculate potential winnings.



4. Winnings and Payouts

Calculating Winnings:
Winnings are calculated based on the odds of the selected participant and the amount wagered. The formula used ensures transparent and fair payouts.


Account Credits:
Winnings will be credited to your account immediately after the race results are confirmed.



5. Fair Play and Integrity

No Cheating:
Any form of cheating, including but not limited to collusion, use of automated bots, or exploitation of system vulnerabilities, is strictly prohibited.


Random Generation:
All races and their outcomes are generated randomly to ensure fairness. Manipulation of race outcomes will not be tolerated.



6. Account Security

Confidentiality:
Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account information. Do not share your login credentials with anyone.


Security Measures:
We implement robust security measures to protect user data. Report any suspicious activity on your account immediately.
